# Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to identify profitability issues, customer behavior patterns, discount impact, regional performance, and operational business insights using Python and SQL.

The objective of this project was to simulate a real-world Data Analytics workflow followed inside organizations where analysts solve business problems using data-driven insights.

---

## Business Problem

The business observed:

- Increasing sales but unstable profitability
- High discounts impacting margins
- Regional performance inconsistencies
- Loss-making products
- Operational inefficiencies

The goal was to identify the root causes behind declining profitability and provide actionable business recommendations.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze sales and profit trends
- Identify high-performing and low-performing regions
- Analyze discount impact on profitability
- Perform customer segment analysis
- Identify loss-making products
- Build KPI-driven business reports

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection
Imported retail sales dataset (Superstore dataset).

### 2. Data Cleaning
Performed:
- Missing value checks
- Duplicate checks
- Data type validation
- Outlier analysis

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Analyzed:
- Sales trends
- Profitability
- Regional performance
- Customer segments
- Product categories
- Discount impact

### 4. KPI Analysis
Created KPIs such as:
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Orders Count
- Average Discount

### 5. Business Reporting & Visualization
Created analytical visualizations and business reports using Python libraries to identify patterns, trends, and operational issues.

---

## Key Business Insights

- Furniture category generated high sales but lower profitability
- Excessive discounts negatively impacted profit margins
- Some regions consistently underperformed
- Technology products generated strongest profits
- Certain products created recurring losses

---

## Business Recommendations

- Optimize discount strategies
- Improve pricing decisions
- Focus on high-margin products
- Improve regional sales strategies
- Reduce dependency on loss-making SKUs
